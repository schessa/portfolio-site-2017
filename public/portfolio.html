mood feed

ui/ux

Mood Feed is an app meant to bring intimacy and simplicity back to social media by allowing users to keep a log of their daily emotions and share it with their friends. In this concept project, I explored UI patterns I’d never seen before, and meditated on the ways that technology can sometimes muddy our communication.

troy camp

brand + print + web + merch

Troy Camp is an organization near and dear to my heart. Having grown up in the neighborhood surroudning USC, I was one of the campers that benefited from their work. When I attended USC, I combined my passion for design with my passion for Troy Camp and became a counselor.
In 2014, I led a diverse team of 8 Troy Camp counselors on a year-long re-branding project, teaching them about identity and design along the way.
